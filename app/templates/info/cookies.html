Cookie Policy

Last Updated: December 7, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how StreamVault uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website and streaming platform.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility. You cannot opt-out of these cookies.

  • Session Management: Maintain your login session
  • Security: Protect against fraud and unauthorized access
  • Load Balancing: Distribute traffic across servers
Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

  • Analytics: Track page views and user interactions
  • Error Tracking: Monitor and fix technical issues
  • Performance Monitoring: Measure page load times
Functionality Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make and provide enhanced, personalized features.

  • Preferences: Remember your language and region settings
  • Personalization: Store your viewing preferences
  • Recommendations: Save content recommendations
Advertising Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements and track the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.

  • Targeting: Show relevant ads based on your interests
  • Frequency Capping: Limit how many times you see an ad
  • Measurement: Track ad performance and conversions

Third-Party Cookies

We may also use third-party services that set cookies on your device, including:

  • Analytics Providers: Google Analytics, Adobe Analytics
  • Advertising Partners: For targeted advertising
  • Social Media: When you share content on social platforms
  • Video Services: For embedded video content

These third parties have their own privacy policies and cookie practices.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control cookies through:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse or accept cookies. However, blocking essential cookies may impact site functionality.
  • Our Cookie Settings: Access cookie preferences in your account settings to manage non-essential cookies.
  • Opt-Out Tools: Use industry opt-out tools like Your Ad Choices for advertising cookies.

Cookie Duration

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them
  • First-Party Cookies: Set by StreamVault directly
  • Third-Party Cookies: Set by other domains

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
